Projects

A selection of applied work and collaborations supporting climate adaptation, public health resilience, and risk-informed humanitarian action.

Futureproofing Health

Center for Climate and Health · Global

I support the development of an international cohort of PhD students from seven countries, building a network focused on climate and health in disaster contexts. My contributions include assessments of government and community drought responses across southern Africa, drawing from national newspapers, government records, and community perspectives.

Payment for Ecosystem Services in Rawanda

Stockholm Environment Institute · Rwanda

As part of a cost-benefit analysis of watershed management in Kigali, I analyzed how upstream investments influence downstream water quality, water access, and related public health outcomes.
